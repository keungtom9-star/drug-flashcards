Small box = 0.04s and 0.1mV
Big box (5 small) = 0.20s and 0.5mV
5 big boxes = 1.0s

🧭 ECG The Basics (Stepwise Read)
  1. Check calibration: 25mm/s and 10mm/mV.
  2. Rate: regular = 300÷large boxes; irregular = QRS in 10s ×6.
  3. Rhythm: regular vs irregular, and is each QRS preceded by P wave?
  4. Intervals: PR (0.12-0.20), QRS (<0.12), QTc (danger if ≥500ms).
  5. Axis & morphology: look for LAD/RAD, BBB pattern, pathologic Q waves.
  6. ST-T changes: elevation/depression, T inversion, hyperacute T waves.
  7. Compare with old ECG and correlate clinically (symptoms, electrolytes, troponin).

🚨 Critical ECG Patterns
Ventricular Fibrillation (VF) SHOCK
Chaotic waveform, no organized P/QRS/T, no cardiac output. Defibrillate + CPR immediately.
Pulseless Ventricular Tachycardia SHOCK
Regular broad-complex tachycardia (usually 120-250 bpm), absent pulse. Defibrillate + CPR.
Torsades de Pointes Mg / SHOCK
Polymorphic VT with twisting axis, often QT prolongation related. Treat reversible causes and give magnesium.
Asystole / PEA CPR
No shockable rhythm. Confirm in 2 leads, start high-quality CPR, adrenaline, and treat 4H/4T causes.
STEMI Pattern
ST elevation in ≥2 contiguous leads (usually ≥1mm limb, ≥2mm precordial). Activate urgent reperfusion pathway.
🛑 Heart Blocks
1st Degree
PR >0.20s (Constant). Regular. Benign.
2nd Degree Type I (Wenckebach)
PR gets longer, longer, longer... then drops QRS.
2nd Degree Type II
PR constant, but random QRS drops. Danger.
3rd Degree (Complete)
P's and QRS distinct and independent. Slow rate. Pacemaker needed.
💓 Common Rhythms & Arrhythmias
Normal Sinus Rhythm (NSR)
Rate 60-100, regular rhythm, upright P before each QRS, fixed PR interval.
Sinus Bradycardia / Sinus Tachycardia
Sinus morphology preserved with rate <60 (brady) or >100 (tachy).
SVT FAST
Regular narrow-complex tachycardia (typically 150-250), P waves often hidden.
Atrial Fibrillation (AF)
Irregularly irregular rhythm, absent organized P waves, variable ventricular response.
Atrial Flutter
Sawtooth flutter waves (commonly ~300 atrial bpm), often 2:1 or variable AV block.
PAC / PVC
Premature atrial beats (narrow) or ventricular beats (wide, bizarre) with compensatory pause.

1. Abdominal Tapping (Ascites) +

Lower: 防水液體膠布 Opsite, 尿袋 Drainage bag, 優碘溶液 Betadine, 火酒 70% Alcohol, 鹽水喉 IV set, 有孔紙杯 Cup with hole.
  • Specimens: 化驗樽(黃蓋) Specimen bottles x3, 標本瓶 EDTA bottle x1.
  • Consumables: 靜脈膠管 Angiocath (16G), 針筒 10ml Syringe, 三頭開關 3-way.
  • Dressing/Support: 防水膠 Tegaderm (大小各3張), 防水墊 Incontinence pad, 壓力膠布 Tensoplast, 膠布 Omnifix, 引流架 Hanger.
2. Acute PD Insertion +
Upper: 手術袍 P6 gown, 無菌手套 Gloves, 無菌布 P31 ward towel, 無菌包 S17 Suture set, 針線 2-0, 急性腹膜導管 Acute PD catheter (loan from 10A).
Lower: 搓手液 Hand rub, 酒精紙 Alcohol pad, 防水紙 Incontinence pad, 消毒藥水 2% Chlorhexidine in 70% Alcohol, 彈性膠布 Elastoplast, 膠布 Zinc oxide, 鉸剪 Scissors.
  • Core items: 針筒 10ml syringe, 針咀 23G needle, 麻醉劑 Lignocaine, 紗布 Gauze.
3. Bone Marrow Biopsy +
Upper: 無菌手套 Sterile gloves, 消毒紗布 Sterile gauze, 骨髓針 Jamshidi bone marrow needle, 無菌包 S1 Biopsy set, 11號刀片 No. 11 blade, 23G needle, 20ml & 10ml syringes, 局部麻醉藥 Local anesthesia.
Lower: 防水液體膠布 Opsite, 防水墊 Incontinence pad, 70% Alcohol, 優碘溶液 Betadine, 彈性繃帶 Elastoplast, 特別標本收集箱 Special specimen transportation box, 刀片盒 Blade flask.
  • Bottles: 標本瓶 EDTA tubes x3, 遺傳學瓶 Cytogenetic bottle x1, 福爾馬林瓶 Formalin bottles x2.
4. Chest Drain Insertion +
Upper: 胸腔引流裝置 Chest drain system, 胸腔引流管 Chest drain catheter, 無菌包 S17 Suture set, 夾子 Tubing clamps, 無菌手套 Gloves.
Lower: 沖洗用水 Water for irrigation, 針筒 10ml syringe, 針咀 23G needle, 紗布 Gauze, 70% alcohol, 局部麻醉藥 2% Lignocaine, 優碘 Betadine, 防水墊 Incontinence pad, 彈性膠布 Tensoplast, 膠布 Zinc oxide.
  • Procedure extras: 縫合針線包 Suture, 刀片 No. 11 Blade.
5. Foley Catheter +
Upper: 無菌包 S4 Catheterization set, 無菌手套 Sterile glove.

  • Box items: 針筒 10ml Syringe, 潤滑劑 KY Jelly, 粉紅色消毒藥水 0.05% Chlorhexidine Gluconate, 清水 10ml Water for Injection.
6. Lumbar Puncture (LP) +
Upper: 無菌包 S10 Lumbar puncture set, 脊髓液壓力計 Manometer, 無菌手套 Sterile gloves, 脊髓穿刺針 Spinal needles.
Lower: 彈性繃帶 Elastoplast, 針筒 10ml syringes, 70% Alcohol, 優碘消毒液 Betadine, 防水液體膠布 Opsite, 防水墊 Incontinence pad, 特別標本收集箱 Special specimen transportation box, 局部麻醉藥 Local anesthesia.
  • Collect: 脊髓液標本樽 CSF bottles.
7. Pigtail Insertion +
Upper: 無菌包 S17 Simple suture set, 無菌手套 Sterile gloves, 接駁喉管 Connecting tube, 套管針引入器 Access needle-trocar style, 導絲 Guidewire, 豬尾引流管 Pigtails (Fr7/Fr8), 血管擴張器 Vascular dilator (Fr7/Fr8).
Lower: 針筒 Syringes (2.5ml x1, 10ml x3, 20ml x1), 尿袋 Drainage bag, 無菌標本瓶 Specimen bottles, 縫線 3-0 Dermalon/Mersilk, 標本收集盒 Specimen collection box, 酒精 70% Alcohol, 防水墊 Incontinence pad, 引流架 Hanger, 優碘消毒液 Betadine, 壓力膠布 Tensoplast, 刀片 No. 11, 局部麻醉藥 Lignocaine.
  • Note: Equipment list aligned with 12 - 豬尾引流管插置用品.
8. Skin Biopsy +
Items: 縫線 Dermalon (3-0, 4-0, 5-0), 無菌包 S17 Simple suture set, 皮膚組織鑽孔器 Skin biopsy punch (4mm), 刀片 No. 11 or 15, 無菌標本瓶 Specimen bottle, 局部麻醉藥 2% Lignocaine, ACP biopsy form.
Requirement: Good lighting + aseptic technique; consent required.
  • Lignocaine: 2% plain or with adrenaline.
  • Suture choice: 3-0 / 4-0 / 5-0 Dermalon depends on site.
  • Specimen must be sent fresh to ACP (No gauze, No saline, No formalin) and do not damage epidermis.
9. Knee Tap (Arthrocentesis) +
Upper: S6 Tapping set (or S17), Sterile gloves.
Lower: Lignocaine, Betadine, 70% Alcohol, Sterile gauze, Elastoplast / pressure bandage.
  • Needles/Syringes: 10ml and 20ml syringes, 18G/21G/23G needles.
  • Specimens: Sterile bottle (C&S), EDTA tube (cell count), plain tube (crystal analysis).
  • Optional: Intra-articular steroid if indicated.
10. Chest Drain Removal +
Upper: S17 Suture set (with stitch cutter), Sterile gloves.
Lower: Betadine / 70% Alcohol, Incontinence pad, Sterile gauze.
  • Dressing: Vaseline gauze (Jelonet) or airtight Tegaderm / Opsite.
  • Technique: Valsalva (hold breath) during tube removal.
  • Disposal: Clinical waste bag and cable ties.
11. Pigtail Removal +
Upper: S17 Suture set or dressing set, Sterile gloves.
Lower: Stitch cutter, Sterile gauze, Betadine / 70% Alcohol.
  • Key step: Ensure the pigtail is unlocked (uncoiled) before removal.
  • Dressing: Tegaderm / Opsite after removal.
12. CVC Removal +
Upper: Dressing set, Sterile gloves.
Lower: Stitch cutter, Sterile scissors (if tip culture needed), Sterile gauze, Occlusive dressing.
  • Position: Supine or Trendelenburg (to reduce air embolism risk).
  • Action: Apply firm pressure for 5–10 minutes.
  • Collect: Sterile specimen bottle if catheter tip culture is required.
13. Shift / Shorten Drain +
Upper: Dressing set, Sterile gloves.
Lower: Sterile safety pin (for corrugated drain), Stitch cutter, Scissors.
  • Dressing: Sterile gauze and ABD pad (if heavy exudate).
  • Check: Re-secure drain and document residual drain length after shortening.
